Seoul Record Store Tour for Record Collector

half day
PLAN
It is a record shop tour that looks around record stores in Itaewon and Hongdae. The course starts from Yeonnam-dong, and tours in Hapjeong and Itaewon. The last schedule ends with a music bar that sells vinyl and plays your recommended songs.
